About Imaqtpie

Michael Santana was born on Febuary 21st, 1992. He is currently living in United States.
Imaqtpie or Michael Santana is an ex-pro player from Team Dignitas from a video game called League of Legends.
After he quit the team, he always wanted to pursue his dream to become a full time streamer on Twitch. Now he is still streaming League of Legends and you can watch him here.

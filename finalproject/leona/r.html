Solar Flare (R or Ultimate Ability)
Cost:100 Mana
Range:1200
Calls down a radiant beam of solar energy dealing 100/175/250 (+80% Ability Power) magic damage and slowing enemies by 80% for 1.5 seconds. Enemies in the center of the flare are stunned instead of slowed.Leona's sword remains charged with Incandescence, causing her next 3/4/5 basic attacks to gain 100 range and deal 30/40/50 (+15% Ability Power) bonus magic damage on hit.

Essemce Flux (W ability)
Cost: 50/60/70/80/90 Mana
Range: 1000
Ezreal fires a wave of energy that deals damage to all enemy champion it passes through for 70/115/160/205/250 (+80% Ability Power) magic damage. If Ezreal or his allied champions are hit by the wave, their Attack Speed is increased by 20/25/30/35/40% for 5 seconds.

Dragon's Rage (R or Ultimate ability)
Cost: No Cost
Range:375
Lee Sin performs a powerful roundhouse kick knocking an enemy champion back and dealing 150/300/450 (+200% bonus Attack Damage) physical damage.Enemies the target collides with are knocked into the air briefly and take physical damage equal to 150/300/450 (+200% bonus Attack Damage) plus 12/15/18% of the initial target's bonus health.

Safe Guard/Iron Will
Cost:50 Energy/30 Energy
Range:700
Safeguard: Lee Sin rushes to target ally. If the ally is a champion, Lee Sin shields the ally and himself for 55/110/165/220/275 (+80% Ability Power) damage for 2 seconds and Safeguard's cooldown is reduced by 50%. After using Safeguard, Lee Sin can cast Iron Will for the next 3 seconds.
Iron Will: Lee Sin gains 10/15/20/25/30% Life Steal and Spell Vamp for 4 seconds.

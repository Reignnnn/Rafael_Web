Sonic Wave/Resonating Strike (Q ability)
Cost:50 Energy/30 Energy
Range:1000
Sonic Wave: Lee Sin projects a discordant wave of sound to locate his enemies, dealing 55/85/115/145/175 (+90% bonus Attack Damage) physical damage to the first enemy it encounters, granting True Sight of the target. If Sonic Wave hits, Lee Sin can cast Resonating Strike for the next 3 seconds.
Resonating Strike: Lee Sin dashes to the enemy hit by Sonic Wave, dealing 55/85/115/145/175 (+90% bonus Attack Damage) physical damage plus 8% of their missing Health (Max: 400 Damage vs. Monsters).
